Bṛhatsam̃hitā of Varāhamihira, KERN, 1865

app1 takes 2 numerical parameters (adhyāya,verse) and displays the pdf page containing this reference.
Example link:
The adhyāya can range between 1 and 107.
For a given adhyāya, the verse ranges from 1 to some value depending on the adhyāya.

This app1 is designed to serve as a link target for references to Bṛhatsam̃hitā that appear in the text of certain dictionaries at the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicon.

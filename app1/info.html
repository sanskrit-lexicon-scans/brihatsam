Bṛhatsam̃hitā of Varāhamihira, KERN, 1865

app1 takes 2 numerical parameters (adhyāya,verse) and displays the pdf page containing this reference.
Example link: https://sanskrit-lexicon-scans.github.io/brihatsam/app1/?11,1 click me!
This link displays the pdf page whose internal page number is 69. On this page, we find the first verse of adhyāya 11.
The adhyāya can range between 1 and 107.
For a given adhyāya, the verses ranges from 1 to some value depending on the adhyāya.

app1 is designed to serve as a link target for references to Bṛhatsam̃hitā that appear in the text of certain dictionaries at the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicon. Notably, the PWG dictionary (Böhtlingk and Roth Grosses Petersburger Wörterbuch) has several thousand such references, and the Cologne displays generate links to app1.
For instance, under the headword gArgIya (गार्गीय) in PWG there is the reference VARĀH. BṚH. S. 11,1. and the displays generate a hyperlink as in the example above.

caveat
It is known that certain hyperlinks for a particular headword are not 'right', in the sense that the headword is not found on the associated app1 page. In the case of PWG, these 'wrong' links typically occur on headwords in the first 4 volumes of PWG; fortunately, some links from headwords in volumes 1-4 are 'right'.
Example 1: चैत्यतरु (cEtyataru) in vol. 2 of PWG refers to "VARĀH. BṚH. S. 32,21." The link 32,21 does not mention चैत्यतरु.
Example 2: जय (jaya) in vol. 3 of PWG refers to "VARĀH. BṚH. S. 27,14". But in our Kern edition, the 27th adhyāya has only verses 1-8. In this instance, app1 will show the default first page of Bṛhatsam̃hitā.

This discrepancy is related to the publication date of this edition of Bṛhatsam̃hitā (1865) and the publication dates 1855-1865 of volumes 1-4 of PWG.
The inference we make is that the 'wrong' references in PWG are based on another pdf than the 1865 version edited by Kern. We have been unable to find a pdf of this missing reference.
